Pathfinder Mod Tracker

What's All This, Then?

Have you ever been playing Pathfinder (or a similar tabletop game) and realized in the midst of combat that there are so many effects in play that you no longer remember what your ability modifiers are at that moment? Don't you wish there was an easier way to keep track of your modifiers when there are spells, barbarian rages, bardic inspiration, and the like all going at once?

Well, look no further!

Simply enter your ability scores in the corresponding fields and hit the "Set" button at the bottom. Your ability modifiers will display in the "Base Mod". Then select the checkboxes for Buffs (positive effects) and Conditions (negative effects) as appropriate. Anything affected by a combat modifier will show the current (new) modifier in the "New Mod"!
